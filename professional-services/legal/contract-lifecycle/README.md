# Contract Lifecycle Management

Contract drafting, negotiation, and execution.

## NAICS Code

**541110** - Offices of Lawyers

## Scope

This model covers contract management, including:

- Contract request and drafting
- Clause library and templates
- Negotiation and redlining
- Approval and execution
- Obligation tracking and renewals

## Key Concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Contract | A legal agreement with terms |
| Clause | A reusable contract provision |
| Negotiation | The revision and approval process |
| Obligation | A tracked commitment from the contract |
