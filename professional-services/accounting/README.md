# Accounting

Public accounting and audit services.

## Scope

This subsector covers accounting firm operations, including:

- Audit engagement management
- Tax preparation and filing
- Client accounting services
- Advisory services
- Compliance and reporting

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `audit-management/` | Engagement planning, fieldwork, and review |
| `client-accounting/` | Bookkeeping and financial reporting |
