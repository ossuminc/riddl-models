# Campaign Management

Marketing campaign planning, execution, and optimization.

## Scope

This model covers campaign management operations, including:

- Campaign planning and calendaring
- Budget allocation and tracking
- Multi-channel execution
- A/B testing and optimization
- Performance measurement

## Key Concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Campaign | A coordinated marketing effort |
| Channel | A communication medium (email, social, etc.) |
| Budget | Allocated spend by campaign |
| Metric | A measured performance indicator |
