# Campaigns

Marketing campaign planning and automation.

## Scope

This subsector covers marketing campaign lifecycle, including:

- Campaign planning and budgeting
- Multi-channel execution
- Marketing automation workflows
- Lead nurturing and scoring
- Campaign performance measurement

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `campaign-management/` | Campaign creation, targeting, and scheduling |
| `marketing-automation/` | Triggered journeys and lead nurturing |
