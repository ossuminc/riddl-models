# Marketing & Advertising

RIDDL models for campaign management, advertising operations, and marketing
analytics.

## Subsectors

### campaigns/
Marketing campaign planning and execution.

**Models:**
- `campaign-management/` - Planning, execution, optimization
- `marketing-automation/` - Journeys, triggers, personalization

### advertising/
Ad buying and operations.

**Models:**
- `media-buying/` - Planning, purchasing, trafficking
- `ad-operations/` - Creative, targeting, reporting

### analytics/
Marketing measurement and audience management.

**Models:**
- `attribution/` - Multi-touch attribution, conversion tracking
- `audience-management/` - Segments, targeting, activation

## Common Patterns

Marketing models frequently use:
- Event sourcing for customer journey tracking
- Projectors for real-time analytics dashboards
- Integration adapters for ad platforms and CRMs
- Process managers for automated campaign workflows

## Integration Points

Marketing models often integrate with:
- CRM systems (customer data)
- Ad platforms (Google, Meta, etc.)
- Email/SMS services
- Analytics platforms
