# Wholesale

Business-to-business commerce and distribution.

## Scope

This subsector covers wholesale trade operations where businesses sell to
other businesses, including:

- Bulk ordering and pricing tiers
- Distribution network management
- Trade credit and payment terms
- B2B customer account management
- Catalog and pricing management

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `distribution/` | Multi-tier distribution and fulfillment |
| `trade-credit/` | B2B payment terms and credit management |
