# Life Sciences

Pharmaceutical and biotech research and manufacturing.

## Scope

This subsector covers drug development and pharmaceutical operations,
including:

- Clinical trial management
- Drug manufacturing and supply chain
- Regulatory submissions
- Pharmacovigilance and safety
- Commercial operations

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `clinical-trials/` | Study design, enrollment, and data collection |
| `drug-supply-chain/` | Manufacturing, distribution, and serialization |
