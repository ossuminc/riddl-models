# Venture Capital

Early-stage investing in startups and emerging companies.

## Scope

This subsector covers venture capital fund operations, including:

- Deal sourcing and pipeline management
- Due diligence workflows
- Investment committee processes
- Portfolio company monitoring
- Fund administration and LP reporting

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `deal-flow/` | Opportunity sourcing, screening, and tracking |
| `fund-administration/` | Capital calls, distributions, and NAV calculations |
| `portfolio-management/` | Company monitoring and value-add tracking |

## Related Patterns

- `patterns/workflow/process-manager/` - For deal evaluation pipelines
- `patterns/entity/aggregate-root/` - For fund/company relationships
