# Supply Chain

Supply chain planning and supplier management.

## Scope

This subsector covers supply chain management, including:

- Demand planning and forecasting
- Supply planning and MRP
- Supplier relationship management
- Procurement and purchasing
- Supply chain visibility

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `demand-planning/` | Forecasting, planning, and S&OP |
| `supplier-management/` | Supplier onboarding, performance, and risk |
