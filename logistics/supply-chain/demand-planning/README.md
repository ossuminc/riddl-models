# Demand Planning

Forecasting, planning, and inventory allocation.

## Scope

This model covers demand planning operations, including:

- Demand forecasting and modeling
- Sales and operations planning (S&OP)
- Inventory optimization
- Allocation and distribution planning
- Forecast accuracy measurement

## Key Concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Forecast | Predicted demand by product and period |
| Plan | Approved supply and demand alignment |
| Allocation | Distribution of inventory to locations |
| Accuracy | Forecast vs. actual comparison |
