# Training Administration

Enterprise training programs, enrollment, and completion tracking.

## NAICS Code

**611430** - Professional and Management Development Training

## Scope

This model covers corporate training operations, including:

- Training program catalog
- Employee enrollment and scheduling
- Attendance and completion tracking
- Compliance training requirements
- Training history and reporting

## Key Concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Program | A defined training course or curriculum |
| Enrollment | An employee's registration in training |
| Completion | Verified finishing of a training program |
| Requirement | Mandatory training based on role or regulation |

## Related Patterns

- `patterns/entity/event-sourced/` - For training history
- `patterns/workflow/process-manager/` - For compliance tracking
