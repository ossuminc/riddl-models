# Corporate Training

Workforce training and development.

## Scope

This subsector covers corporate learning operations, including:

- Training program administration
- Competency management
- Learning paths and curricula
- Instructor-led training
- Compliance training

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `training-administration/` | Programs, enrollment, and scheduling |
| `competency-management/` | Skills, assessments, and gaps |
