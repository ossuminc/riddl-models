# Agriculture

Farming operations and agricultural management.

## Scope

This subsector covers agricultural production, including:

- Crop planning and management
- Livestock tracking and health
- Farm equipment and operations
- Irrigation and soil management
- Harvest and yield tracking

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `farm-management/` | Fields, crops, and operations |
| `livestock-management/` | Animals, health, and breeding |

- `patterns/entity/aggregate-root/` - For farm hierarchies
