# Oil & Gas

Upstream and midstream petroleum operations.

## Scope

This subsector covers oil and gas exploration and production, including:

- Well planning and drilling
- Production optimization
- Pipeline operations
- Reservoir management
- HSE compliance

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `well-management/` | Drilling, completion, and production |
| `pipeline-operations/` | Flow management and integrity |
