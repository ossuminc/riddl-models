# Field Operations

Job site management and field execution.

## Scope

This subsector covers on-site construction operations, including:

- Job site management
- Equipment tracking and utilization
- Daily logs and reporting
- Safety compliance
- Time and attendance

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `job-site-management/` | Site setup, access, and daily operations |
| `equipment-tracking/` | Asset location, utilization, and maintenance |
