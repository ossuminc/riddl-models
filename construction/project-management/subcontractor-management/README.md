# Subcontractor Management

Subcontractor contracts, compliance, and payment processing.

## Scope

This model covers subcontractor operations, including:

- Prequalification and selection
- Contract and scope management
- Insurance and compliance tracking
- Progress and payment applications
- Lien waiver management

## Key Concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Subcontractor | A contracted trade partner |
| Contract | Terms, scope, and schedule |
| PayApplication | A request for progress payment |
| Compliance | Insurance, licensing, safety requirements |
