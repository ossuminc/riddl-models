# Life & Annuity Insurance

Life insurance, annuities, and long-term savings products.

## Scope

This subsector covers insurance products with long-term obligations and
investment components, including:

- Life insurance underwriting
- Annuity contract management
- Beneficiary administration
- Policy loans and surrenders
- Actuarial valuations
