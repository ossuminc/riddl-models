# Reinsurance

Insurance for insurers and risk transfer mechanisms.

## Scope

This subsector covers the transfer of risk from primary insurers to
reinsurers, including:

- Treaty and facultative placements
- Cession and retrocession
- Bordereaux reporting
- Claims recovery
- Portfolio management
