# Food Service

Restaurants, catering, and food service operations.

## Scope

This subsector covers food and beverage service businesses, including:

- Restaurant floor and kitchen management
- Menu and recipe management
- Order taking and fulfillment
- Catering and event planning
- Food cost and inventory control

## Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `restaurant-operations/` | Table management, orders, and kitchen display |
| `catering-management/` | Event bookings, menus, and logistics |

## Related Patterns

- `patterns/workflow/process-manager/` - For kitchen workflows
- `patterns/entity/event-sourced/` - For order tracking
