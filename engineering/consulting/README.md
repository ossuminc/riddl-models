p# Consulting

Engineering consulting and professional services.

## Scope

This subsector covers engineering consulting operations, including:

- Client engagement management
- Project staffing and allocation
- Technical knowledge management
- Deliverable tracking
- Expert review and QA

| `knowledge-management/` | Technical expertise and reusable assets |
